Doppler effect

efecto Doppler

The Doppler effect is the percieved change in the frequency of a signal due to the change in speed with respect to a certain observer. We listen a lot to it, for example when a car passes trough. The bigger is relative speed more evident is its effect. This effect is not only about sound. In fact, we know the universe is expanding because of the light from the stars being a little redish. While playing infectoDoppler you have to imagine how a sound frequency changes as coming from a moving asteroid. You can try the animation embed here touching the red button. The blue one allows you to simulate different trajectories.
