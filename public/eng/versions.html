iD: versions

free virus

The virus begins to attack the population g4g and no one offer resistance. The society doesn't take any contingency measures to stop it, perhaps because the virus is not fatal. 212 of the 233 sounds get infected. The peak of the curve takes place in day 39 when 126 sounds are heard at the same time.

second wave

The virus begins to attack the population g4g. This time the society restrict the circulation a little during 90 days. For a while the sounds believe that the outbreak is over but they realize that the immunity period for recovered sounds is 34 days. Nobody can stop the second wave.

lock down

When the virus gets strong in the population g4g society decides to restrict normal circulation by a 55% during 40 days. While this contingency measure is in place the outbreak is under control but it speeds up as soon as the restriction is released. 200 of the 233 sounds get infected. The peak of the curve takes place in day 78 when 74 sounds are heard at the same time.
