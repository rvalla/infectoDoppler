a musical game?

During the decade of 1950 musicians as John Cage in America or Karlheinz Stockhausen in Europe introduced aleatory elements in their works. While I was studying this techniques I wrote the butterfly and its chaotic effect (a game for 3 people, 1 piano and 13 ping pong balls). A musical piece which is built through the rules of a game and doesn't require extended musical training.
