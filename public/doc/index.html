Documentation

Here I intend to share information about infectoDoppler and the tools needed to build a version. infectoDoppler is open source, so you can dive into its repository too.

Browsers and audio

It is posible that your browser doesn't work correctly during the game. It was difficult to find an approach which works in Android, iOS, Windows, macOS and Linux. Here are some things you can try...

player guide

The infectoDoppler simulation guides the players and records their sounds simultaneously. It is written in p5js and it can be congigure passing parameters through url.

composition

To build a version of infectoDoppler a computational model simulates an epidemic and merge infected sounds together. It is written in python3 and can be configure through a .json file.
